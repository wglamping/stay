How far is Wander from Dallas?

Approximately 90 minutes by car, depending on your starting point and traffic. Wander is located near Paris, Texas.

Which stay should I choose?

Choose Cabin SOL for a design-forward romantic escape for two with a private cedar hot tub and sauna. Choose the Bali Dome for substantially more interior space, two bedrooms, and room for up to four guests.

Is Cabin SOL private?

Yes. SOL has its own private outdoor living area, cedar hot tub, glass-front sauna, fire feature, and outdoor shower surrounded by the East Texas landscape.
